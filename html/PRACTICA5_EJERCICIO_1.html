EXAMPLE 6.2 Compact Trigonometric Fourier Series of a Periodic TriangleWave Find the compact trigonometric Fourier series for the triangular periodic signal x(t) shown in Fig. 6.4a, and sketch the amplitude and phase spectra for x(t).
